Our team

We are all passionate about combining tech with global health, social good, and equity in order to fight the pandemic.

Collaboration

We are a team of over thirty enthusiastic and ambitious youth and students from 22 universities: California Northstate University School of Medicine, Cornell, Duke, Federal University of Paraíba (Brazil), Harvard, Harvey Mudd, King's College London, Lambda School, La Rioja International University (Spain), Northeastern, Presbyterian University of Mackenzie (Brazil), Princeton, Sophia University (Japan), Stanford, UC Irvine, UCLA, UCSF School of Medicine, UPenn, University of Houston, University of Oxford, Western University (Canada), and Wilfrid Laurier University (Canada). We’re also worldwide, with members originally from or studying in 12 countries - Brazil, Canada, Colombia, India, Japan, Malaysia, Nigeria, Peru, Singapore, South Korea, the UK, and the USA - giving us a broad global reach.
